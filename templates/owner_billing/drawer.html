Why this matters

Cash variance > {{ '%.0f'|format(alert_pct) }}% of the expected total triggers a warning. Patterns to watch for:

  • Recurring shortages on a single shift — coach the staff member.
  • Large overages — usually missed bill entries; check the audit log.
  • Round-number variances (₹100, ₹500, ₹2000) — usually a missed sale, not theft.
