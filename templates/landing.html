Common questions

If your question isn't here, mention it in the request form below.

Do my customers have to download anything?

No. They scan the QR code on their table with their phone's camera, and the menu opens in their browser. That's it.

Do I need any hardware or new equipment?

No. Any phone, tablet, or laptop with a browser works for your kitchen / counter screen. We help you print waterproof QR code cards for each table.

What does it cost?

Free for the first 30 days. After that, pricing depends on your café's size and table count — typically a small monthly fee well below what a single missed order costs you. We discuss this on the onboarding call before you commit.

Can I customise the look and feel?

Yes. Your café's name, logo, and brand colour appear on the customer-facing menu, the receipts, and confirmation emails.

Is my customer data safe?

Yes. Passwords are encrypted, logins are protected by lockout and optional two-factor authentication, and every form is rate-limited. We're transparent about our security at /.well-known/security.txt.

Can multiple staff use the system at once?

Yes. You can create separate logins for managers, kitchen staff, and counter staff, each with their own access level. Every action is logged for audit.

What happens if my Wi-Fi goes down mid-order?

Our checkout endpoint protects against duplicate orders even if the customer's phone retries the request. Once their device reconnects, the order goes through cleanly — no duplicates, no missed orders.

How long does onboarding take?

Typically 2–4 days from approval. We handle digitising your menu (PDF, photo, or even handwritten — anything works) and printing QR codes; you only need to give us 15 minutes for the walk-through.
